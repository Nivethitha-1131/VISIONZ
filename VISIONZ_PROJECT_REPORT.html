📊 Executive Summary

VISIONZ is an enterprise-grade AI-powered video quality control system designed for FMCG manufacturing environments. It automates product defect detection in real-time using advanced computer vision and machine learning technologies.

🚀 Business Impact:
  • 90% Time Reduction: Inspection time reduced from 2+ hours to 3 minutes per batch
  • Multi-Model AI: Combines YOLOv8 detection with Claude AI and Llama2 analysis
  • 17 Specialized Classes: Covers structural, surface, labeling, color, and component defects
  • Enterprise Security: JWT authentication, bcrypt encryption, rate limiting
